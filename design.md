# Design System Document

## Theme Overview
Our design system is built on a `light` color mode, ensuring clarity and readability.

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Utilizes `plusJakartaSans` for a modern and impactful presence.
- **Body Text**: Employs `beVietnamPro`, chosen for its excellent legibility and versatility in various contexts.
- **Labels**: Features `inter`, providing a clean and functional typeface for interactive elements and small text.

## Color Palette
The brand's identity is anchored by a curated color palette:
- **Primary Color**: `#3264FF` serves as the dominant brand color, ideal for primary actions and key brand elements.
- **Secondary Color**: `#0F294D` acts as a supporting color, providing depth and contrast without overpowering the primary hue.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#FF9913` is an accent color, bringing a vibrant touch for highlights, alerts, or decorative elements.
- **Neutral Color**: `#ADAEB3` provides a foundational neutral tone, perfect for backgrounds, borders, and subtle UI elements.

## Shape and Spacing
- **Roundedness**: A moderate roundedness of `2` is applied, offering a friendly yet professional aesthetic to UI components.
- **Spacing**: A normal spacing level of `2` is maintained, balancing information density with visual comfort and breathability.

## Component Specific Colors
- **Primary**: The primary color, often used for main interactive elements. Value: `#3264FF`.
- **Secondary**: A secondary accent color for less prominent UI components. Value: `#0F294D`.
- **Tertiary**: An additional accent for specific highlights or decorative elements. Value: `#FF9913`.
- **Neutral**: The base neutral color for backgrounds and non-chromatic elements. Value: `#ADAEB3`.
- **Success**: Indicates successful actions or states. Value: `#4CAF50`.
- **Warning**: Highlights potential issues or requires user attention. Value: `#FFC107`.
- **Error**: Signifies critical errors or destructive actions. Value: `#F44336`.
- **Info**: Provides informative feedback or guidance. Value: `#2196F3`.
